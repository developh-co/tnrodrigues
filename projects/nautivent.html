Nautivent

Industry

Nautical Tourism / Experiences

Client

Nautivent (via Developh)

Service

Web Design, Development, Booking Strategy, Photography

Date

2024

Nautivent is a nautical tourism brand offering premium boat tours and sea experiences along Portugal’s west coast. The goal was to create a digital experience that mirrors the elegance and serenity of their services while streamlining user navigation and booking paths.

The website was designed and developed in Framer, with a strong focus on performance, mobile responsiveness, and storytelling through visuals. The visual identity uses navy gradients, calm transitions, and minimal typography to reflect the premium nature of the brand.

A custom CMS was implemented to allow the client to independently manage tour pages, seasonal changes, and customer content. We also launched a blog to support SEO and content marketing efforts, positioning Nautivent as an authority in nautical tourism.

The structure is future-proofed for scalability and booking system integrations, ensuring the platform grows alongside the business. Acess it here.
